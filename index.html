Quick Tips

  • Consistency beats intensity — gentle effort most days wins
  • If you're holding your breath, you're pushing too hard
  • Sharp pain = stop. Mild stretch discomfort = good
  • Morning stiffness is normal — you'll loosen up by the end
  • Check progress every 2–3 weeks: deeper squat? Flatter wall angels?
